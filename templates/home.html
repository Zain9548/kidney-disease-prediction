What is Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)?

Kidney Function

Kidneys filter waste, control blood pressure, and maintain body balance.

CKD Progression

CKD develops gradually and can lead to kidney failure if untreated.

Early Detection

AI-based prediction helps detect CKD at early stages.
